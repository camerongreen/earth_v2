Fishes

Fish - The good

The term fish describes a vast array of completely different species, almost as broad as the term animal describes.

From the often maligned gold fish, who's memory has been proven time and time again not to be 5 seconds as popular mythology ascribes to it, to larger fish who have been shown to remember for long periods where to avoid predators, there is a range of cognitive abilities we are only beginning to understand. In a well known defense of the gold fish, one ingeneous person taught them to play a simple form of soccer as an example of their learning abilities.

Fish like all other animals enjoy pleasure, a study into why fish would come to "cleaning stations" where smaller fish would eat parasites from the skin of larger fish, found that tickling a fish with a soft instrument produced the same behaviour in the larger fish.

Like other animals, fish have been shown to be able to use tools, have complex social structures, exhibit cooperation and other individual personality traits.
