Geese - The good

Geese are from a large and ancient family of birds including ducks and swans. The domestic goose is a domesticated grey goose, a species present from the subarctic to more temperate regions.

Geese undertake some amazing migrations, and have been seen flying at tremendous heights, with some species crossing over the Himalayas in their annual migrations of thousands of miles.

Geese mate for life, which can be well over 40 years, and are monogamous.
