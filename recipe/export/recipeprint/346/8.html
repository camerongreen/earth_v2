Spicy Carrot Cake

Prep time: 5 minutesCooking time: 55 minutesTotal time: 1 hour

Description

This is a great cake, that's easy to make, tasty and pretty healthy

Ingredients

1 1⁄2 c
Carrot (Grated)
1 1⁄2 c
Raisins (Raisins are large dark grapes, smaller white grape ones which are also called raisins in the US we call sultanas)
2 1⁄2 c
Water
3 T
Vegetable oil
1 c
Golden syrup (If you can't get it a pale treacle would be a good replacement)
2 t
Cinnamon
2 t
Nutmeg
2 t
Mixed spice (Or allspice)
3 c
Wholemeal self raising flour
1 c
Walnuts (Chopped into smallish pieces)
1 pk
Soy cream cheese (We buy it in a tub here)
1⁄3 c
Icing sugar
1
Lemon (The rind is what we want)
1 t
Vanilla essence

Instructions

Place carrots, raisins, water, vegetable oil, golden syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg and mixed spice in a pan, and simmer for 10 minutes. Allow to cool completely (good to do the day before)

When cool add flour and walnuts.

Mix all together well, and pour into a well buttered cake tin (or 2 cake tins)

Bake at 180 degrees for 45 mins to an hour. (If putting into 2 tins, they need less baking time). To test if cooked, poke with a stick of raw spaghetti, and if it comes out clean without cake batter on it, it is ready. Or just give it a push with your finger, if it bounces back, it's ready.

Beat remaining ingredients together well, and ice cake with it.
